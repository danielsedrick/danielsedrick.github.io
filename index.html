Graphic Design Skills

This first logo is one I made for a fictional company called DropIt!. The idea behind DropIt! was to create an online suggestion drop box where users could leave suggestions or feedback to other users. In following with the idea, I created the logo to look like a suggestion box with a note being dropped into it. This is just one variation of the logo design.

This next image wasn’t for any specific project or assignment, but instead just for fun! To get more experience with programs such as Photoshop and Illustrator, I set to work trying to bring some of my creativity to life through an image. Making this helped me learn some foundational skills such as developing an eye for design, working with new and unfamiliar programs, and keeping myself focused on a project of my choosing.
