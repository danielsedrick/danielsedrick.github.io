Main Fork of the Salmon, Idaho

The Salmon River at our Idaho location offers the best entry level adventure! This trail is full of exciting twists and turns. As well, it has plenty of shoreline to pull over to set up a campfire, a picknick or even to pitch tents and campout!

This river is open to families, soloists, and groups looking to have a good time out on the moving water. This river is exciting enough to keep experienced river-goers on their toes, but if you don't have any experience don't let that stop you! We offer river guides who can teach you proper rowing techniques and help you conquer any of our rivers!

Benson Hill River, Wasatch Canyon

More interested in taking your time and enjoying the natural beauty of Wasatch Canyon? The Benson Hill river gives floaters the chance to experience different paces of rafting. With some sections of rapids, a few of sharp twists and turns, and even lazy stretches to casually cruise through, the Benson Hill river keeps things interesting the whole way through. This 86 mile trail can take 6 days to finish, so pack a snack! For those who instead want to take a trip for just a couple days can take advantage of our various entry and exit ports to shorten the trip.

Desolation River; Desolation, Utah

Strap in, because this river is a wild ride! The Desolation River sure doesn't get its name for a smooth experience. This river provides an adrenaline rush for even the most experienced enthusiests. For those seeking a whitewater rush and a seriously good time, the Desolation River delivers.

This river is restricted for those 14 and up. Experienced individuals and groups are invited to brave the rushing water on their own, and to set their own pace through the raging rapids. For those feeling adventurous or lacking experience, we provide river guides and raft aids to help you conquer the waters.
