Weather Web Browser

Initial Planning Document

Purpose Statement

The purpose of this website is to provide accurate weather reports for different locations across America. We plan to show users the information they are looking for through visually appealing ways. The website will offer users inforamtion about temperature, precipitation, wind speed and direction, as well as other facts related to weather and geography specific to their location.

Audience Analysis

The target audience for this website is people in their 20’s-40’s. Typically, they are “adults” who are either living in these locations, or are seeking to travel to these locations. They people who probably prepare ahead, and look ahead for information concerning their day, which means they will want details about their specific location readily available.
